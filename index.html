About Us

Jeevan Prabodhini Educational Institution is a private college located in Sangli, Maharashtra, India, accredited by AICTE. We strive to bring higher education to the doorsteps of economically disadvantaged students.

Vision: To provide affordable quality education while equipping students with knowledge and skills in their chosen streams.

Mission: To create an environment that builds a foundation for lifelong learning and makes female students financially capable through higher education.
